Traditional Food (Balanced & Human Style)

Laksam

A comforting dish from Kelantan and Terengganu, Laksam features rolled rice noodles in a creamy fish-based coconut gravy. Topped with herbs and sambal, it’s rich, hearty, and full of traditional East Coast flavors.

Onde-Onde (Kuih Buah Melaka)

Onde-Onde are soft, chewy rice balls filled with palm sugar that melts in your mouth. Coated in fresh grated coconut, these bite-sized sweets are a festive favorite, bringing a little burst of joy with every bite.
