Welcome to Malaysia MADANI

Welcome to the official website for Malaysia’s 68th National Day celebration! This year’s theme, “Malaysia MADANI: Rakyat Disantuni” emphasizes compassion, mutual respect, and the well-being of the people in building a harmonious nation.

Malaysia is a country rich in culture, traditions, and heritage. Among our most treasured symbols of unity is traditional food. From the nation’s pride, Nasi Lemak, to the sweet delight of Onde-Onde, each dish reflects the unity and diversity of our multicultural society.

This website showcases some of Malaysia’s beloved traditional dishes such as Nasi Kerabu, Satay, Laksam, and Onde-Onde, meals that not only satisfy the appetite but also connect people across different races and religions.

Let us celebrate Malaysia’s 68th National Day with pride and gratitude, while appreciating our traditional food heritage that continues to strengthen our national identity.
